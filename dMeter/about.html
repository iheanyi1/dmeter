About dMETER

About Us

Our Mission:

To sustainably incentivize people working together to create systems of decentralized monitoring, reporting and verification (dMRV) for regenerative actions.

Our Vision:

All regenerative action on Earth is tracked and understood, publicly viewable and accessible, rewards and incentives are delivered to the creators of regenerative value.

Our Values:

Respect, Kindness, Empathy, Openness, Curiosity, Legitimacy, Positivity, Creativity, Imagination. Be Biased To Action Over Discussion.

Benefits Of dMeter Way:

  1. Inspires entrepreneurship that expand dMeter way.
  2. Allows for standardization, trustlessness, immutability, transparency, and open accessibility.
  3. Data collectors own and benefit from their data which engenders security of data.
  4. This spur local communities to venture into more regeneration actions.

Why dMeter?

  1. Centralize methodologies of Credit creation which impairs implementation.
  2. Few regenerative projects have the financial resources to implement or comply centralized methodologies.
  3. Current methologies lack proper monitoring.
  4. High cost of entry and as such hinders scalability.
